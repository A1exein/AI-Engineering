### Private Equity Markets Research

#### Current Market Conditions Summary
- Deal flow has declined moderately, with valuations stabilizing after peak activity.
- Exit environment remains favorable with high-profile IPOs and strategic M&A deals occurring.

#### Key Trends and Drivers
- Dry powder levels remain strong but fundraising activity has slowed down.
- Sector focus shifting towards technology and healthcare opportunities.

#### Risk Factors and Opportunities
- Market volatility could affect future deal-making and valuations.
- Opportunities exist in distressed assets, especially in travel and hospitality sectors.

#### Performance Metrics
- Average EBITDA multiples have decreased to approximately 10-12x.

#### Outlook (6-12 months)
- Anticipated continued interest in tech-related deals and potential growth in secondary buyouts.
