### Real Estate Markets Research

#### Current Market Conditions Summary
- Commercial real estate shows continued softness, while residential markets remain robust.
- Rising interest rates impacting financing conditions across sectors.

#### Key Trends and Drivers
- Shift towards remote work affecting commercial real estate demand, particularly office space.
- Geographic trends show growth in suburban areas countering urban decline.

#### Risk Factors and Opportunities
- Economic slowdown could lead to further declines in commercial valuations.
- Opportunities in multi-family and logistics sectors with ongoing demand.

#### Performance Metrics
- REITs have seen average returns of around 3-5% over the past year versus direct investment returns of 8-10%.

#### Outlook (6-12 months)
- Expected stabilization in residential but continued pressure on commercial, especially in office space.
