## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Portfolio-Level Risks

#### **Concentration Risk**
- **Risk**: Over-allocation to alternative investments
- **Mitigation**: Diversification across asset classes, vintages, and geographic regions
- **Monitoring**: Quarterly rebalancing reviews

#### **Liquidity Risk**
- **Risk**: Limited liquidity in private investments
- **Mitigation**: Staggered investment timeline and maintaining liquid reserves
- **Target**: Maintain 10-15% in liquid alternatives and public markets

#### **ESG Implementation Risk**
- **Risk**: "Greenwashing" or inadequate ESG compliance
- **Mitigation**: Robust due diligence and ongoing monitoring
- **Framework**: Third-party ESG verification for all investments
