### Appendix B: Key Metrics Definitions

#### Performance Metrics
- **Net IRR**: Internal rate of return net of all fees and expenses
- **Net Multiple**: Total value to paid-in capital ratio, net of fees
- **Excess Return**: Portfolio return minus benchmark return
- **Sharpe Ratio**: (Return - Risk-free rate) / Standard deviation of returns

#### Risk Metrics
- **VaR (Value at Risk)**: Potential loss at 95% confidence level over 1-month period
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline in portfolio value
- **Correlation**: Statistical measure of portfolio correlation to public market indices
- **Beta**: Measure of portfolio sensitivity to market movements

#### Alternative Investment Specific Metrics
- **Combined Ratio (Reinsurance)**: (Losses + Expenses) / Premiums * 100
- **J-Curve Effect (Private Equity)**: Initial negative returns followed by positive returns over time
- **Cap Rate (Real Estate)**: Net operating income / Property value
- **Occupancy Rate**: Percentage of occupied rental units
